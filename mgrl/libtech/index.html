Libtech Funding Project

Category : MGRL Excel Vendor relations

In order to receive important chunks of funding (i.e., libtech and census), the IT department at MGRL was tasked with creating a viable purchasing plan for patron-focused items so that we may later be reimbursed.

There were several stipulations encompassing vendor & item limitations. It was the current department head's first time completing this project, which gave us both many opportunities to learn the ropes of funding acquisition.

I am very proud of the work I did regarding this project. I put in a lot of time communicating with vendors, requesting and juggling quotes, thoroughly documenting every transaction, and concurrently trying to use the money effeciently so that every item bought has a distinct purpose in the system.

This project strengthened my abilities in professionally communicating with vendors and department heads, working with a budget under strict circumstances, and managing enormous spreadsheets.

The screenshots above are of the Excel workbook, designed wholly by me, that kept track of all the money that was allocated and spent for every branch. It has been altered for confidentiality of the organizations involved, but I hope it illustrated the dedication I have to making important data more accessible and understandable.
